Who we are

Priority America is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization focused on helping American Veterans translating military experience into marketable employment skills in the IT field.

What We Do.

Military values such as integrity, loyalty, team work, accountability, and leadership are some of the most desired employee traits. However, returning veterans face immense challenges in successfully transition these experiences to the business world. Programs such as TAP only provide basic introductory information for career opportunities, the education marketplace has overwhelmingly amount of information that lacks clear direction, and many employment programs often do not focus on high paying technology careers.

Priority America is created to identify and provide the solution to these challenges.

Career Roadmaps

Step-by-step career roadmaps for some of the most sought after technology jobs today. Whether it's coding, cloud engineering and architecture or cyber security. We will show you how to get there. Following a familiar process similar to military training, ie. bootcamp, SOI/MOS, MCT, and etc.

Vetted Training Partners

Returning veterans are often targeted for their GI Bill by for-profit entities. While not all for-profit institutions are scams, many do not deliver the promised value or employment opportunities. We connect you with institutions that offers the best training, for relevant skills, and with the best results.

Connect with Hiring Authorities

Opprotunities to interact directly with hiring managers from Government and industry. We organize intimate events and information sessions for vets to learn first hand what opportunities are available, what skills are in demand, and how you can get hired.

Real life Experience

Profession jobs require work experience. After successfully completing training programs, you can practice what you have learned in real life situations with Priority America. You are fully immersed into a team of working professionals delivering real projects. This team environment enforces what you have learned, but also help you develop the soft skills required to be successful in a professional environment.
